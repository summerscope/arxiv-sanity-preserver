About fairXiv

A friendlier UI to tame the torrent of papers from arXiv

fairXiv attempts to:

  1. Filter the arXiv feed for papers related to ML fairness and ethics
  2. Provide a more usable and accessible UI for scanning arXiv papers

Paper query

The script searches for specific sub categories of computer science on arXiv, and then matches at least on of these terms:

Categories

  • cs.CV
  • cs.AI
  • cs.CL
  • cs.CY
  • cs.LG
  • cs.NE
  • stat.ML

Terms

  • fairness
  • ethics
  • ethical
  • safety
